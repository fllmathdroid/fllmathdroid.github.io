Meeting Format

  • 9:30am - 10am: Early birds work together.

  • 10am - 10:15am: Beginning Talk - Each member to stand up and tell the team:
    1. "What did I do last time?"
    2. "What is my plan for today?"
    3. "Is there any blockers that I need help with?"
    4. Optional: "I have a special topic to present". (<5 min)

  • 10:15am - 11:45am: Work Together.

  • 11:45pm - noon: Ending Talk - Each member to stand up and tell the team:
    1. "What did I do today?"
    2. "What is the hardest part? What is the most fun part?"
    3. "Who did I work with and what can I learn from him/her?"
    4. "Is there anything need help?"
    5. "What do I plan to do before next meeting?"
