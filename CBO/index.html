CBO- Ideas for robots and attachments

Main Robot-

The main robot all our attachments go on- Right now, we want to put 3 large motors(2 to power our wheels) and a medium motor. For sensors, Marvin and I chose 2 color(that's definite, to align the robot to the board), 1 gyro(to help with accurate turns), and we currently are still deciding between touch or ultrasonic. If you have any comments or suggestions, please tell them to me, thanks :D

THE DOG GEAR SYSTEM

With something called the dog gear system: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NZbt3tnySyI we can score more points because this allows us to quickly attach/deattach attachments which are connected to the motor, even when the motor is still running. I made a small sample of this: the thing at the right is just an example attachment. We're most likely going to use this on our medium motor on the main robot, but it still depends.(Maybe the large motor as well)
