  Game Board and the Missions

    
    
    
  Mission 01 - Using Recycled Material

    
 Using Recycled Material: In the BROWN
    Scoring: Green Bin containg at least one matching Yellow or Blue Bar, all from the other team, is completely in your Saftey.
    Value: 60 points per bin in either safety for each scoring bin in either safety, the other team gets the points too, and vise versa.

    
  Mission 02 - Methane

    
 Methane: In the BLACK
    Scoring: Methane is in the Truck's engine compartment, and/or the Factory's 
    Value: 40 points per methane. 
    Leniency: Full/exact nesting is not required. (It means it doesn't have to be perfectly in.

    
  Mission 03 - Transport

    
 Transport: In the GREEEN
    Value: 50 points if the Truck suppports all the Yellow Bin's weight.
    Balue: 60 points if the Yellow Bin is completely east of the Truck's Guide.

    
  Mission 04 - Sorting

    
 Sorting: In the LIGHT BLUE
    Scoring: Yellow/blue bars are in their matching green bin (bin scores independently)
    Value: 60 points per bin if completely in the other team's Safety, by way of your West Transfer. (same as M01)
    Value: 7 points per bar if completely in your West Transfer area and/or completely on your West Transfer.
    6 points per bar if never completely in your West Transfer Area.
    Method Constraint: These require sequence/path as described, in addition to final positions.
    Value: 8 points per black bar for part of a scoring Flower Box, or in thier original Setup position.
    3 points per black bar in their matching Green Bin, or in the Landfill Bin.
    -8 points anywhere else.
    Method Constraint: Bars must only enter Green Bins directly from the Sorter's east chute or with the Lego People in the Sorter Area.

    
  Mission 05 - Careers

    
 Careers: In the BLUE
    Scoring: Move at least one person to the Sorter Area to earn a helpful exception to the Rules.
    Value: 60 points 
    Leniency Bonus: Team technicians area allowed to unclog any east chute blockage by hand, and/or put mis-sorted bars into their correct
    bin, including bars that didn't land in any bin.

    
  Mission 06 - Scrap Cars

    
 Scrap Cars: In the RED
    Scoring: Fix the old Car by installing the Engine/Windshield, or fold the Car and sell it for scrap. (Scores only one way)
    Value: 65 points if the Engine/Windshield unit is installed in the unfolded Car in the proper space and direction.
    50 points if the Car is completely folded and completely in the East Transfer Area.
    Leniency: Full/exact nesting is not required.
    Method Constraint: The Car must never cross into Safety, even partly.
